# ApeCoin Airdrop Tracker
A Node.js application to track and monitor unclaimed ApeCoin airdrops for Bored Ape Yacht Club (BAYC), Mutant Ape Yacht Club (MAYC), and Bored Ape Kennel Club (BAKC) NFT collections.
## Overview
This project interacts with the Ethereum blockchain to identify NFTs from the Ape ecosystem that have not yet claimed their ApeCoin airdrop. It scans through the entire collection of NFTs and generates reports on unclaimed tokens, along with their current market value.
